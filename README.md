LF/HS Weekly Intelligence Brief — EMEA
Weekly competitive intelligence for Life Fitness / Hammer Strength EMEA field sales reps.
Live: https://trentraleigh89.github.io/Market-Intel-Brief-EMEA/
What this is
A web-based weekly briefing for EMEA field reps. Same shell and editorial structure as the US dashboard, sourced from EMEA-relevant outlets:
Verticals: Health Clubs, Hospitality, Multi-Unit Housing, Performance / Athletics, and Sport Science / Performance Education (the EMEA rename of US Campus Recreation)
Tag vocabulary: EMEA-specific tags (DISCOUNT, BTR, FOOTBALL, MID-MARKET) layered on the global taxonomy
Competitor coverage: Same eight global competitors as US, plus Panatta and gym80 (EMEA-relevant strength manufacturers)
Source priorities: EMEA trade outlets (Health Club Management, EuropeActive, Euronews, Travel And Tour World, ESSMA, Premier League)
What's in this repo

Key Account Treatment in EMEA
The global key account list (Planet Fitness, Life Time, Gold's Gym, SNAP, EoS Fitness, Crunch Fitness) applies to EMEA as well. These six brands have minimal EMEA presence and are not field-rep-callable in any region.
EMEA's largest operators (Basic-Fit, PureGym, The Gym Group, Virgin Active, David Lloyd, McFIT/RSG Group, Holmes Place, Fitness First Germany) are NOT on the global key account list and DO appear as regular vertical insights in EMEA briefs.
EMEA may internally classify these large operators as "priority accounts" or another tier — that naming does not affect dashboard logic.
How to use
Click any insight card to flip and reveal the competitive implication and seller conversation starter
Filter chips at the top: All, Trending, Saved, the five verticals, and Competitive
Bookmark icons save insights for later reference
Helpful row captures brief-level feedback
Region selector in the hero navigates to the US dashboard
Print button for hard-copy view
Week picker archives prior weeks
Analytics
Pageviews and custom events tracked via Plausible (privacy-focused, no cookies). EMEA engagement is reported separately from US.
